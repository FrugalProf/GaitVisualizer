Gait Cycle Analysis Tool

Step 1: Download & Load Diagram
Download the standard Gait Cycle image here:
Download Figure 2.1 Image
(Source: Figure 2.1 from Whittle's Gait Analysis, 5th Edition)
Then, click the center circle on the screen to upload it.

Step 2: Enter Data
Start in the highlighted box at the top (First Frame of IC). Enter the Video Frame # for each event. Use Tab to move to the next event automatically.

Step 3: Analyze
Verify the FPS (Default: 240). The tool will calculate durations (red tags) and generate the Phase Plot.

Step 4: Refine Images
You can drag the small event images to center them, or scroll over them to zoom in/out.

Step 5: Save
Use the "Save as JPG" button in the top bar to download a snapshot of your analysis.

Tool developed with credit to Professor Fedel.
